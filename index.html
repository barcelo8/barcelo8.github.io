Why Travel by Amtrak?

Why is it better than flying?

There are no extra fees for traveling on a train. The seats are much more comfortable on a train than on a plane. And instead of heated up food on a plane, the food is made on board.

Why is it better than driving?

There is no maps, no traffic, or having to worry about anything, just sit back and relax. Your back won't hurt from sitting in a seat with thin cushions for a long period of time.

Why is it better than a bus?

There is no food served on a bus. And, you can't stand up and walk around unless you are using the restroom. The bathrooms are more dirty on a bus. And, more cramped space on a bus. And, you can't get any fresh air until your destination stop.

Why is it better to travel by train?

Food is made on board. There are big luggage racks in each car. Also, unlike airline coach seats, the coach seats on a train have huge space between each seat, and you get a big window. You are able to walk around the train and stretch your legs at a fresh air stop. You get large picture windows compared to on a bus, car, or plane. There are showers on board, but they are only for Sleeping Car passengers. As you can see, there are many reasons why it is better to travel by train.
